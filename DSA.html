This is DSA's website

A San Diego based non-profit working within their local communities to improve data usage for the good of the people. Check out their good work to see ways you might improve your data usage.

I've been working with this team for quite a while now. It started as an extraction of their current website from another platform that just wasn't meeting their needs. We threw in a few tweeks and improvements. And now we're working together on a completely new website.

Working with them has been enjoyable - it's almost like being their employee rather than having them as a client. We have weekly video check-ins and share resources where appropriate. They have their own design team and editors so its great to see them all each week and be able to rely on them for consistent feedback and project assets.

This is my biggest project to date and it takes a lot of my time here and there but there are slower times too - while I'm waiting on the designs to pour in - where I'm working on projects for other clients as well.
