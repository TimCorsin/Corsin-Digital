This is DSA

A San Diego based non-profit working withiin their local communities to improve data usage for the good of the people.

I've been working with this team for quite a while now. It started as an extraction of their current website from another platform that just wasn't meeting their needs. We threw in a few tweeks and improvements. And now we're working together on a completely new website.

Working with them has been enjoyable - it's almost like being their employee rather than an agency contractor. We have weekly video check-ins and share resources where appropriate. They have their own design team and editors so its great to see them all each week.

This is my biggest project and it takes a lot of my time here and there but there are slower times for them, while I'm waiting on the designs to pour in, where I'm working on other projects as well.
